CLEARSPEND PRODUCT BRIEF
Regional Banking Launch Packet
Version: 1.0 | Updated: 2026-04-08

1) Product Objective
ClearSpend is a personal finance dashboard for regional bank customers. It helps customers track spending, set budgets, and manage savings goals while giving the bank clearer engagement signals for retention and cross-sell.

2) Target Users
- Primary: Digitally active retail customers, ages 25-44
- Secondary: Relationship managers and branch advisors supporting financial wellness conversations

3) Scope for Phase 1 (Q3 2026)
- Category-based spend breakdown (monthly and weekly)
- Budget setup and overspend alerts
- Savings goals with progress tracking
- Subscription detection alerts
- Personalized spending insights based on historical patterns

4) Not in Scope for Phase 1
- Credit score simulation
- Investment portfolio recommendations
- Small business expense management
- Open API partner marketplace

5) Launch Timeline
- Design sign-off: 2026-05-10
- Engineering freeze: 2026-06-20
- Risk and compliance readiness review: 2026-06-28
- Pilot launch (employees + friendly users): 2026-07-08
- Public launch target: 2026-07-28

6) Dependencies
- Mobile app release train capacity (critical)
- Notification service latency below 5 seconds
- Compliance approval for behavioral alert wording
- Vendor data enrichment contract finalization
